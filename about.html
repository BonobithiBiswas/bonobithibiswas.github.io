"Art is my refuge and my adventure. It is what I go home to, it is what I leave home for."

A multidisciplinary Indian visual artist based in Paris, Bonobithi works in diverse analogue and digital media to create multisensory storytelling experiences. Dealing chiefly with mixed reality, drawing and painting, photography, poetry film, sculpture, audio art, performance, multilingual writing and visual poetry, she constructs immersive multimedia installations. The thematic focus of her practice is the South Asian post-colonial historic-cultural landscape, identity of the female queer body of colour, and intercultural dialogue through shared safe spaces. She is interested in speculative fiction and psychogeography in upholding silenced narratives of vulnerability in marginalised communities, particularly among women. Having participated in various artist residencies and group and solo exhibitions internationally, Bonobithi’s works have been extensively displayed in cities across India, Indonesia, Italy, UK, Spain, Belgium, Greece, Iceland, Brazil, Germany, Iran and France.
