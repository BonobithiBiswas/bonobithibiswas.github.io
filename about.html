"Art is my refuge and my adventure. It is what I go home to, it is what I leave home for."

A multidisciplinary Indian visual artist currently based in Paris, Bonobithi is currently pursuing her Master in Fine Arts in Transdisciplinary New Media at Paris College of Art, Paris. She works in diverse visual media including multimedia installation, mixed reality, poetry film, video essay and painting.
The thematic focus of her practice is migration, the South Asian post-colonial historic-cultural landscape, identity of the female queer body of colour and its relationship to surveillance and intercultural dialogue. She is interested in psychogeography in the context of porous borders movements, delving into perceptions of belonging and its causations in various migrant communities, particularly among women. Having participated in various artist residencies and group and solo exhibitions internationally, Bonobithi’s works have been extensively displayed in cities across India, Indonesia, Italy, UK, Spain, Belgium, and France.
