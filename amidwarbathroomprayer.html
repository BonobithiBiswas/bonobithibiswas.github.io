A Mid-War Bathroom Prayer

Cinepoem
2024

This cinepoem explores the female queer body of colour, situating it in current politics, drawing upon notions of identity and objectification. The female body becomes the easiest target in times of political strife and war, as is apparent in the current Israeli occupation of Palestine and the ongoing genocide in Gaza. This artwork is the psychogeographical interpretation of a safe space for such a body. It is a hope to reclaim space, strength and room for vulnerability in these torn times.
